Brazilian Jiu Jitsu Algueirão - Mem Martins Master the gentle art

Unity BJJ is a family-oriented training center for the grappling art of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu located in Algueirão-Mem Martins,Portugal Unity BJJ was founded by the renowned black belt, Cyborg, and is proudly a part of the Unity Jiu-Jitsu Team. We are dedicated to bringing top-quality state, national, and international instructors to the academy to teach, improve, and evolve Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu in Algueirão - Mem Martins and beyond.

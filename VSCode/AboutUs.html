Who we are

Unity BJJ is more than just a gym—we are a community dedicated to Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, personal growth, and excellence on and off the mats. Our mission is to provide world-class instruction in a supportive and competitive environment, helping students of all levels develop their skills in self-defense, fitness, and sport Jiu-Jitsu. Whether you're a beginner looking to build confidence or a competitor aiming for gold, Unity BJJ is the place to elevate your game.

Founder

Professor Roberto "Cyborg" Abreu, founder of Unity BJJ, is a world-class black belt and multiple-time IBJJF and ADCC champion. Known for his aggressive style and innovative techniques, Cyborg has trained and competed at the highest levels. His teaching philosophy focuses on strong fundamentals, strategic techniques, and building a supportive community where students of all levels can thrive.
